Key Terminology

Hydro Jetting
A high-pressure water technique used to clear stubborn blockages and maintain clean sewer lines.
Slab Leak Detection
Advanced methods to locate hidden water leaks beneath a building's foundation.
Sewer Camera Inspection
Technology used to visually inspect underground pipes and detect issues like blockages or root intrusion.
Trenchless Sewer Replacement
A minimally invasive technique to replace damaged sewer lines without extensive digging.

Common Questions (FAQ)

1. How can I detect a leak in my plumbing system?

Leak detection can be performed using tools like slab leak detection and sewer camera inspections to pinpoint the exact location of a leak without causing unnecessary damage.

2. What is the process for trenchless sewer replacement?

Trenchless sewer replacement involves the use of modern technology to replace damaged sewer lines without the need for extensive excavation, preserving landscaping and reducing disruption.

3. How often should I have my plumbing system inspected?

It is recommended to have plumbing systems inspected at least once a year to identify potential issues before they become serious problems.
